AI Chatbot — Widget Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable chat widget. Look at the bottom-right corner!

How to embed on your website:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget.js" data-url="https://your-domain.com"></script>

Customization options:

  • data-url — Your chatbot URL
  • data-position — "right" or "left"
  • data-color — Bubble color (hex)

Click the purple chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to try it out!
